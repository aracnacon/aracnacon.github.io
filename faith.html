Defining My Faith

Defining my faith is like following Bilbo's path in Lord of the Rings. Along the way I have had no idea where I was going, if I would make it or would I recognize myself at the end. It all starts so innocent and pure, we have little awareness of the challenges we will face. Years pass and yes, the path was jagged, missing from much of our life. We no longer resemble the innocence we started with. The soul has been corrupted beyond recognition.

And yet, Jesus is with us always. Loving us more than we could possibly love ourselves. Always watching, waiting for my love for him. Without God we would be nothing, without Jesus's redemption we would be lost to sin.

"I can do all this through him who gives me strength."

— Philippians 4:13
