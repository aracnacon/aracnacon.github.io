My Story

I joined the Marines mainly because I needed direction, a target to focus on. College for me was just a repeat of High School, which I struggled with. The Marines gave me a challenge like none other. Plus, I was going into a higher end MOS. I needed something to excel at. In the Marines I got to display my grit, my toughness. My will to complete everything that was thrown at me. I was even able to help others along the way as brothers in the Marines do.

"Semper Fidelis!"
